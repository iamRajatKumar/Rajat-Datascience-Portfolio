Rajat in a Nutshell

Full-Stack Developer turning Data Scientist — blending engineering, analytics, and storytelling.

I’m Rajat Kumar, a software engineer who loves building things that actually get used. For the past few years, I’ve been working as a Full-Stack Developer, crafting web applications with Angular, React, Spring Boot, Microservices, and AWS. I enjoy taking ideas from a rough sketch to a production-ready system — clean APIs, reliable services, and polished UIs.

Over time, I realised that the products I’m most proud of all have one thing in common: they help people make better decisions with data. That’s what pushed me toward Data Science & Machine Learning. Now I’m intentionally transitioning — learning Python, statistics, ML, and MLOps, and building end-to-end projects that go beyond models to solve real business problems.

Most of my learning happens outside “formal” classrooms. I experiment with datasets, follow online courses, read blogs, and implement what I learn in side projects. I enjoy turning these projects into blog posts and tutorials so others can reuse my code, learn faster, and avoid the mistakes I made along the way.

In the long run, I want to sit at the intersection of engineering, analytics, and product — building systems that are not just technically solid, but also explainable, ethical, and useful to the people who rely on them.
